Recommended Ad Strategy

+

Quick recommendation: Start with Facebook Ads for your side business. It's cost-effective and ideal for local outreach in Hamilton.

Why Facebook?

  • Lowest average cost per click (~$0.93 CAD)
  • Popular among local Hamilton competitors
  • Supports visual storytelling and testimonials
  • WhatsApp/Messenger integration for quick client responses

Future Expansion

Once your Facebook campaigns show consistent results, consider:

  • Google Display Ads (for broader awareness)
  • Instagram Reels (for visual engagement)
  • LinkedIn (for high-income clients and group insurance)

Comparing Platforms

Even modest investments on Facebook can yield meaningful results. Align your monthly budget with your business goals.

How Often to Run Ads

+

Regular investment in Facebook ads improves ad learning and reach. Start with shorter durations, then adjust based on results.

Getting Started

  • Run initial campaigns for at least 7 days
  • Facebook's algorithm needs time to learn and optimize
  • Start with daily budgets of $10-$20 CAD

What to Monitor

  • Click-through rate: Are users engaging?
  • Cost per click: Is it within budget?
  • Conversions: Are you getting leads?

Scaling Strategy

After a successful 7-day campaign:

  1. Review results in Ads Manager
  2. Scale budget by 20-30% for successful ads
  3. Pause underperforming ads
  4. Test new audiences with small budgets

Remember: Advertising is an evolving process. Regular reviews and optimization are key to success.
